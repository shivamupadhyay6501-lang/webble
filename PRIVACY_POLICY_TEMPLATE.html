Privacy Policy for Webble

Last updated: December 3, 2024

Introduction

Webble ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Don't Collect

Webble does NOT collect, store, or share any personal information, including:

  • Your name, email, or contact information
  • Device identifiers or location data
  • Search history or query logs
  • Any personally identifiable information (PII)

How Webble Works

When you ask a question in Webble:

  1. Your query is sent to our backend server
  2. The server searches the web using DuckDuckGo
  3. Results are processed by an AI model (HuggingFace)
  4. The answer is returned to your device
  5. Nothing is stored or logged

Third-Party Services

Webble uses the following third-party services to provide functionality:

DuckDuckGo Search API

Used for web search. DuckDuckGo does not track users or store search history.
Privacy Policy: https://duckduckgo.com/privacy

HuggingFace Inference API

Used for AI-powered answer generation. Search results are processed but not stored.
Privacy Policy: https://huggingface.co/privacy

Data Transmission

Your search queries are transmitted over HTTPS (encrypted) to our backend server. The server processes your query in real-time and does not retain any data after sending you the response.

Permissions

Webble requires the following Android permissions:

  • INTERNET - Required to search the web and get answers
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE - To check if you're online

We do not request any other permissions.

Children's Privacy

Webble does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The app is suitable for all ages as it does not collect personal data.

Data Security

While we don't store your data, we take security seriously:

  • All communications use HTTPS encryption
  • No user accounts or authentication required
  • No cookies or tracking mechanisms

Your Rights

Since we don't collect or store your data, there is no data to:

  • Request access to
  • Request deletion of
  • Request correction of

You can use Webble with complete privacy and anonymity.
